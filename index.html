FAQs!

Got questions? We've got answers!

Who can attend?

This event is for girls and non-binary teenagers aged 13-18 who are interested in exploring tech through their passions!

What if I have never coded before?

No experience, no problem! This event is beginner-friendly and we'll have workshops and mentors to help you learn and build something amazing :)

How much does it cost to attend?

Nothing! The event is completely free to attend.

What should I bring?

Just your laptop and yourself! If you don't have access to a laptop, please indicate that on the registration form as early as possible.

Where is the event located?

Downtown Orlando - we're working on finalizing the perfect venue and we'll let attendees know as soon as possible!

About Synthesis

Synthesis is an Athena Initiative hackathon by Hack Club—youth‑led, hands‑on, and focused on shipping. We empower girls and non‑binary students to explore the intersection of technology and their passions, building real projects that matter to them and their communities.
